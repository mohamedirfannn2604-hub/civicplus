About CivicPulse

Built for the People, by the People

CivicPulse is a citizen-first platform that bridges the gap between communities and the services they need — from emergency response to public infrastructure complaints.

Our mission is to make government services accessible, transparent, and efficient for every resident — regardless of digital literacy or physical location.

Transparency — Every complaint tracked in real time
Speed — Emergency response under 5 minutes
Inclusion — Accessible for elderly and differently-abled users
Free Forever — No fees, no subscriptions
